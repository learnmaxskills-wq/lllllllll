Week 1

Find The Right Startup Idea

Students stop guessing and start researching what is actually in demand.

  • Use AI to study market demand and audience pain points

  • Define the startup concept, positioning, and direction

Week 2

Build The Product

Students create something real using AI-assisted workflows, even without coding experience.

  • Build a website, app, ecommerce store, or game
  • Shape the user journey and startup interface
  • Launch a live product students can actually show
Goal: have a live product with real founder energy behind it
Week 3

Market The Startup

The product gets attention through AI-generated content and short-form marketing assets.

Goal: create a startup that looks launch-ready, not hidden

  • Automate support, lead flow, and startup operations
  • Package the business into a founder-ready narrative
  • Demo the startup and open the path to advanced selection
