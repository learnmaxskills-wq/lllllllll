02
FOUNDER TRACK
ADVANCED PROGRAMME
After Demo Day, top performers are selected for the advanced Founder Track. This is not for everyone. This is for the students who proved they can execute, think, and ship. They spend 90 days scaling their startup to real users, real revenue, and investor readiness.
How Selection Works
After Demo Day, LearnMax evaluates every student on four criteria — product quality, presentation confidence, business logic clarity, and vision for growth. The top 30–40% are personally invited to apply for Founder Track. Applicants submit a one-page founder statement outlining their growth plan. Final selection is based on founder potential and commitment — not just product sophistication. Selected students receive a formal invitation letter, which becomes the first thing they show their parents.
Month 1 · Days 31–60
Scale — Get Your First 100 Real Users
The product exists. Now it needs real people using it. Students learn customer psychology, acquisition strategy, and how to measure what matters. Every session focuses on one thing — growing the number of real people engaging with their product.
Week 1: Customer research — who are they, where do they live online, why would they use this
Week 2: Acquisition — organic growth, paid ads, referral systems, community building
Week 3: Analytics — install tracking, understand what users do, what makes them leave
Week 4: Iteration — change one thing based on data, measure impact, repeat. First 100 users milestone.
Month 2 · Days 61–90
Revenue — Make Your First Rupee
Users exist. Now monetize. Students learn pricing psychology, how to build a sales funnel that converts, and how to handle a real financial transaction with a real customer who is not a friend or family member.
Week 1: Pricing strategy — value-based pricing, anchoring, why free is a trap
Week 2: Sales funnel design — landing page, offer, checkout, thank you sequence
Week 3: First revenue push — direct outreach, launch campaign, conversion tracking
Week 4: Unit economics — cost of acquisition, lifetime value, sustainable model. First revenue milestone.
Month 3 · Days 91–120
Pitch Ready — From Startup to Investment Case
Revenue exists. Now tell the story in a way that makes investors write cheques. Students learn how investors think, what they look for, how to build a pitch deck, and how to perform under pressure in a real investor meeting.
Week 1: Investor psychology — what they look for, how they decide, what kills deals
Week 2: Pitch deck construction — 10 slides, each with one job to do
Week 3: Pitch delivery — voice, pacing, confidence, handling hard questions
Week 4: Mock investor sessions — real pressure, real feedback. LaunchPad application submitted.
Ongoing · Full Duration
Community — The LearnMax Founder Network
Founder Track students are not alone. They have access to weekly cohort calls, peer accountability pods, direct mentorship, and introductions to the LearnMax investor and industry partner network.
Weekly: Founder cohort call — wins, blocks, accountability
Bi-weekly: Peer review session — students critique each other's progress
Monthly: Mentor session — industry leaders, founders, investors
Ongoing: Private Slack group, resource library, investor introductions
